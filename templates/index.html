Audio Tips

  • Volume and bass controls only respond when audio is actively playing — the speaker ignores them when idle
  • Both the Mac system volume and the speaker's hardware volume affect loudness independently — if sound is too quiet, check both
  • AUX usually sounds better than Bluetooth
  • Bluetooth may distort at high volume
  • Bass has ~15 adjustment steps

Permissions & Troubleshooting

  • Bluetooth: macOS may ask for permission
  • Mac media keys require Accessibility access
  • If the status bar shows "Scanning…" for a long time, unplug and replug the speaker's power cable to reset its state
